Privacy Policy for FAM Sync

Last updated: 2025-07-18

Thank you for using FAM Sync! We value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. What Information We Collect

  • Authentication Cookie from the boat rental website, which allows us to access your bookings in your name.
  • Booking Information including booking dates, boat details, and other info displayed on your booking page.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Access Your Bookings: The authentication cookie is sent securely to our server. We use it only to retrieve your bookings page and parse your booking information.
  • Sync Bookings: Your booking data is used to update your selected Notion database and calendar via the Notion API.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • To Notion: Your booking details are sent to your Notion account using the official Notion API, in order to create or update records in your chosen database and calendar.

We do NOT sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal data with any other third parties.

4. Data Security

Your data is transmitted via secure HTTPS connections. We do not store your authentication cookie or booking data beyond the duration necessary to complete the synchronization process.

5. Data Retention

We retain your booking data only temporarily as needed to sync to your Notion account. After synchronization, all copies of your personal data and authentication cookie are deleted from our servers.

6. Your Choices

  • No Data Storage: We do not keep your booking information or authentication information once synchronization is complete.
  • Revoke Access: You may disconnect the extension and revoke access to your Notion account at any time.
